Jungle Restaurant

Vegetarian specialities From the World to You, since 2003

Typical vegetarian dishes from five continents including shakshuka, pasta cacio & pepe, savoury timatim, ratatouille, to name a few, are part of the joyful menu offered by Jungle Restaurant. Choose to have your lunch with us: bring a friend and get a 25% discount!

He learned the art of local cuisine, choosing to take home the authentic recipes handed down from generation to generation. After 20 years, our chef has returned home to bring the flavours of the world directly to our table. Enjoy!
